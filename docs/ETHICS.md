# Ethical Considerations

Customer churn analysis should be used to support fair and transparent retention decisions.

## Privacy

- Do not upload unnecessary personally identifiable information.
- Remove phone numbers, email addresses, payment information and government identifiers.
- Use anonymized customer IDs where possible.

## Fairness

- Avoid using protected attributes to unfairly target or exclude customers.
- Compare outcomes across relevant customer groups.
- Investigate whether churn scores disproportionately affect certain groups.

## Human Oversight

- Treat dashboard insights as decision support, not final decisions.
- Review high-risk customer lists before taking action.
- Provide customers with fair and respectful retention offers.

## Data Quality

- Validate missing values, duplicate records and date fields.
- Document how churn and risk groups are defined.
- Reassess thresholds when business conditions change.
